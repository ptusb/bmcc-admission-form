## Step-by-Step Deployment Guide

### Step 1: Create Netlify Account
1. Go to **https://app.netlify.com/signup**
2. Click "Sign up" 
3. Choose one of these options:
   - Sign up with **GitHub** (recommended - easiest)
   - Sign up with **GitLab**
   - Sign up with **Email**
4. Complete the sign-up process (it's free!)

### Step 2: Deploy Your Site (Drag & Drop Method - Easiest!)

**Option A: Direct Drag & Drop** (Fastest - No Git Required)
1. After logging in, you'll see the Netlify dashboard
2. Look for the section that says **"Want to deploy a new site without connecting to Git?"**
3. **Drag and drop your entire project folder** onto that section
   - Drag folder: `d:\admission form UI project`
   - Or click "Browse to upload" and select all files:
     - index.html
     - style.css
     - script.js
     - logo.jpg
     - README.md
4. Netlify will automatically upload and deploy (takes 10-30 seconds)
5. **Done!** Your site is now live! üéâ

**Option B: GitHub Integration** (For easier updates later)
1. First, push your code to GitHub:
   - Create a repository on GitHub named "admission-form"
   - Push your files to the repository
2. In Netlify, click **"Add new site"** ‚Üí **"Import an existing project"**
3. Choose **"Deploy with GitHub"**
4. Authorize Netlify to access your GitHub account
5. Select your repository: `admission-form`
6. Click **"Deploy site"**
7. **Done!** Your site is now live! üéâ

### Step 3: Get Your Live URL
Once deployed, Netlify gives you a URL like:
```
https://random-name-123456.netlify.app
```

You can customize this URL:
1. Go to **Site settings** ‚Üí **Site details**
2. Click **"Change site name"**
3. Enter your preferred name (e.g., `bmcc-admission-form`)
4. Your new URL: `https://bmcc-admission-form.netlify.app`

### Step 4: Share Your Form Worldwide! üåç
Your admission form is now live and accessible from anywhere in the world!
- Share the URL with students
- Add it to your website
- Share on social media

## How to Update Your Site Later

**If you used Drag & Drop:**
1. Go to your site dashboard in Netlify
2. Click **"Deploys"** tab
3. Drag and drop your updated files
4. Site updates automatically!

**If you used GitHub:**
1. Push changes to your GitHub repository
2. Netlify automatically detects and deploys changes!

## Bonus: Custom Domain (Optional)
Want to use your own domain like `admission.bmcc.edu`?
1. Go to **Domain settings** in Netlify
2. Click **"Add custom domain"**
3. Follow the instructions to connect your domain
4. Netlify provides free HTTPS for custom domains too!
